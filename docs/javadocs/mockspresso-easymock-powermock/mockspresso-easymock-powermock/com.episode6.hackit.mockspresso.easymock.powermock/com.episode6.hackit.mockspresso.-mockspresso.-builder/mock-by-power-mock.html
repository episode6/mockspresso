mockByPowerMock

fun Builder.mockByPowerMock(): Builder

Applies the com.episode6.hackit.mockspresso.api.MockerConfig for Powermock + EasyMock. Requires your project have a dependency on org.easymock:easymock v3.4, org.powermock:powermock-api-mockito2, and org.powermock:powermock-module-junit4 v1.7.0+. Also requires your test be runWith the org.powermock.modules.junit4.PowerMockRunner
